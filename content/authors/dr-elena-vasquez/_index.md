Dr. Elena Vasquez covers the cutting edge of science, space exploration, and emerging technologies. With a PhD in Astrophysics, she translates complex scientific discoveries into engaging, accessible journalism.

Her beat spans NASA missions, space industry developments, breakthrough research, and the intersection of science and policy. She has reported on major space milestones and provided expert commentary on the commercialization of space.
