David Kiprop reports on global diplomacy, peace and conflict, and international development across Africa and the Middle East. His coverage includes UN proceedings, African Union summits, and grassroots peacebuilding initiatives.

With a background in conflict resolution and political science, David provides nuanced reporting that goes beyond headlines to explore the human impact of geopolitical events. His work has been cited in policy discussions across multiple continents.
