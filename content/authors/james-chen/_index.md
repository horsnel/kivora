James Chen serves as MenshlyGlobal's Senior Geopolitical Analyst, covering US foreign policy, trade sanctions, and global energy markets. With over a decade of experience in international relations and financial markets, James provides readers with deep strategic analysis of geopolitical risk and its impact on global markets.

His expertise spans Middle East diplomacy, US-China trade dynamics, and emerging market sovereign risk. Prior to joining MenshlyGlobal, James worked as a risk consultant for multinational corporations operating in high-stakes regulatory environments.
