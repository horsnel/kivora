Dr. Fatima Al-Hassan provides expert analysis on global health trends, pharmaceutical developments, and healthcare policy. Her background in epidemiology and public health gives her a unique lens on everything from drug approvals and clinical trials to pandemic preparedness.

She covers the intersection of health, economics, and policy, with particular focus on healthcare access in developing nations and the business of pharmaceutical innovation.
