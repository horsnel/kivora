title: "Amara Okonkwo"
role: "West Africa Correspondent"
---

Amara Okonkwo covers West Africa's rapidly evolving economic landscape, with deep expertise in fintech, digital infrastructure, and regional trade agreements like AfCFTA. Based in Lagos, she brings on-the-ground reporting and analysis of the innovations driving Africa's next chapter of growth.

Her reporting combines data-driven market analysis with compelling human stories, making complex economic trends accessible to global audiences. She has covered major developments in Nigerian and Ghanaian tech ecosystems.
